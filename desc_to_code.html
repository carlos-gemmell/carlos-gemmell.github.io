Desc to Code

AI is clearly going to be a big part of our future. So much so that lots of us are pretty concerned with it replacing us. Right now we get to choose what direction we take as humans given that AI is of such use to us. I believe in a future where AI is a companion rather than a substitute for humans.

By uniting AI with humans we supercharge ourselves, able to be more creative, work faster and tackle harder problems. Desc to Code is the latest realisation of my vision applied to programming.
